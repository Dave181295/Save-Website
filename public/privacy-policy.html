LifeLink-AI, Inc. ("we", "us", "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Save app (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. What We Collect

2. What We Don’t Collect

When you save public content using the App, such as posts or stories from social platforms, that content is stored securely in cloud storage provided by Google Cloud. This content is encrypted and linked to your internal user ID. It is never made public or reused by us.

Content is retained for up to 45 days unless manually deleted by you.

These services may process data as governed by their respective privacy policies.

We do not sell your data. We only share limited data with Google services for operational purposes (e.g., login and analytics). We may disclose information if required by law or legal process.

Your account and any associated saved content may be deleted from within the app at any time. Content is automatically removed from storage after 45 days if not accessed. We do not retain data beyond what is necessary to provide the service.
